Wearable Precision Healthcare

PROTXX integrates wearable sensors and advanced data analytics into non-invasive clinical tools that address unsolved challenges presented by neurodegenerative medical conditions in which multiple physiological systems are impaired. Identifying and quantifying these impairments is difficult but critical for diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation.

Healthcare delivery and research teams in North America, Europe, and Asia are leveraging the PROTXX solution to improve the management of neuro-degenerative medical conditions with many different causes, including age-related disorders (stroke), injury (concussion), disease (Parkinson’s), and invasive procedures (neurosurgery).

Introducing the PROTXX Physiological Tremor Sensor

  • Athletic
  • Clinical
  • Industrial
  • Military

The tiny mass, high sensitivity, and unobtrusive head-mounted design allow the PROTXX PTS to detect both normal and pathological physiological tremor signals reflecting postural stabilization, mechanocardiographic motion, and intracranial pulsatility.

PTS signals contain rich feature sets that enable quantitative assessments of impairments to multiple physiological systems: neurological/CNS; nervous system (afferent, efferent); sensory (visual, vestibular, somatosensory); musculoskeletal; and cardio/neuro-vascular.

PTS assessments help classify healthy vs. impaired individuals over a wide age range and quantify impairments to multiple physiological systems based on a one-minute, non-invasive standing postural stability test that can be carried out in any athletic, clinical, industrial, or military environment.

PROTXX Platform as a Service Solution

  • Precision Sensor
  • Mobile App
  • Data Analytics

The PROTXX mobile app allows PTS tests to be administered by non-expert operators in less than one minute to a single test subject or simultaneously to multiple test subjects.

PROTXX cloud-based data storage, analytics, and reporting services utilize the PTS signals to support classification of complex medical conditions based on quantitative assessments of underlying impairments.

The PROTXX PTS, mobile app, and data services are integrated into a platform-as-a-service solution and licensed to healthcare delivery and research teams. Current customers include public and private healthcare, sports medicine, and physical/occupational therapy.
